Subtly

            Smiles on faces, pain in braces

            Separate beings going places
            
            Random impulse, steady paces
            
            Start of a forever full of phases
   
            
            Smiles and hope, thoughts elope
            
            Texts per minute, the texts explode
            
            All experience must run through Cole
            
            Filtered reaching, lightened soul
   
            
            Smiles forever, even on darker paths
            
            Silence is not always harsh
            
            Sometimes soft, removes pain from the past
            
            Simple like this it will last
            
       
            Saved me, Tamed me, Raised me
            
            Not all loves are called baby.

- Mia
